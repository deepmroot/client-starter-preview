Core platform features

Listings marketplace

Users can create public item listings with images, descriptions, condition, category, and estimated trade value.

Swap offer engine

Buyers propose an exchange item and add optional cash when item values differ.

Negotiation chat

Each swap has a direct chat thread so both sides can refine terms before accepting.

Cash balancing

Support structured top-up amounts inside the platform for uneven item values.

Trade states

Offers move through sent, negotiated, accepted, shipped, delivered, and completed states.

Shipping support

Generate labels and track logistics after a trade is confirmed.

How the exchange flow works

01List item
User publishes an item and desired swap preferences.
02Send offer
Another user proposes an item and optional cash top-up.
03Negotiate
Both users chat, refine terms, and align value.
04Confirm trade
The exchange is accepted and logistics are triggered.
05Ship & close
Shipping labels are created and the transaction is completed.

Product notes

  • Trust and safety will be central for item exchange flows.
  • Escrow may be needed if cash top-ups are processed in-platform.
  • Carrier integration can start as a service abstraction, then connect to APIs later.
  • A website-first launch is the fastest route unless mobile is mandatory from day one.
